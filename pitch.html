Pitch is best described as the frequency of a note that is on an audible spectrum from the lowest to the highest pitches. Everytime you hear a single note's tone, you are hearing the note's pitch! A blend of pitches makes a song, where we have described them as musical notes. Believe it or not, some people can actually tell what note is being played based on the pitch they hear alone.
